There is no god but Allah. Muhammad is the messenger of Allah.

The Shahada (Arabic: الشهادة‎ aš-šahādah [aʃ.ʃaˈhaːda] (About this sound listen), "the testimony"; aš-šahādatān (الشَهادَتانْ [aʃ.ʃahaːdaˈtaːn], "the two testimonials"); also Kalimat aš-šahādah [كلمة الشهادة [ˈkalɪmat-], "the testimonial word"]) is an Islamic creed declaring belief in the oneness of God (tawhid) and the acceptance of Muhammad as God's prophet. The declaration, in its shortest form, reads (right to left in Arabic): لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا ٱلله مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُولُ ٱلله lā ʾilāha ʾillā llāh muḥammadun rasūlu llāh There is no god but God. Muhammad is the messenger of God.
